OUR ENTERPRISE

Meraki Enterprise is a student-run business at Quezon City University, dedicated to creating high-quality, delicious, and nutritious candy. Our mission is to provide a unique and personal touch to our products, putting our hearts and souls into everything we make.

MISSION

To create an exceptional blend of rich taste and impeccable texture for our customers, which is why we make extraordinary treats that incorporate top-quality nuts. We're committed to providing an excellent and indulgent experience, one nutty treat at a time.

VISION

To be the No. 1 leading Candy Manufacturer in Quezon City, providing people with high-quality products and exceptional customer service.

VALUES

-To help the economy by providing new products for job opportunities that support the continuously evolving and improving economy cycle.

-To provide high quality products that even people with limitations on sweets can eat.
